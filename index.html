InternZ score estimator for the Monash MBBS, beta one?




To estimate a z-score from subject marks: Complete the MED2000 and MED4000 boxes only. This will also estimate a equivalent percentile for your mark

To calculate a z-score from a percentile in the cohort: Fill out the bottom input only

This estimator uses a statistical mark:percentile model based on some data collected from current Year 5D students - thank you for your contribution!
